ABOUT XPARROTS

The XParrots will be a collection of 10,000 hand illustrated characters to be minted on the XRP ledger. Randomly assembled, every one of the XParrots will be exclusive, epic & unique! Each XParrot will be incredibly cool like their future owners.

VISION

Our vision for XParrots is not only to create an amazing and unique NFT integrated into the XRPL, but to make XParrots a launch pad in which we can release future NFT projects to build our community as we aim to create a metaverse.

We plan to collaborate with other NFT projects to become a core hub of the XRPL with an engaging and friendly community.

MISSION

Our mission is to build a community of like-minded people starting with the XParrots project. As a community member of XParrots you will have access to many unique and beneficial collections/projects that we plan to release in the future. Our plan is to deliver our roadmap and reward those members who stick with us as we grow within the XRPL ecosystem.

LET THE XPARROTS FLY!

The XParrots stand out in the NFT world with their fun, lovable and colourful traits. They are social birds and don’t like to miss out on all the XRPL fun!

By combining our love for the amazing Parrots with the power of NFTs, we hope to create the XParrots’ community to bring people together and to reward members with future NFT projects!

The XParrots will have around 50+ attributes of which will be used to build each NFT. We will showcase NFT attributes on a regular basis as our generator creates amazing XParrot results.

THE XPARROTS NFT MINT

Ripple and their partners are currently working on the functionality to make minting the NFTs possible on the XRPL. The most likely solution proposed is the (XLS-20d) that introduces the ability to complete minting transactions, burning and trading of NFTs on the XRPL.

Once the NFTs technical infrastructure is operational, the XParrots’ project team will setup and enable the ability for our $XParrot token holders to mint their corresponding XParrots NFT.

The $XParrot token is considered an IOU which gives its owner the option to exchange one token for 1 NFT when the NFT’s are launched. Tokens will be burned in the minting process.

CHARITY PROGRAMS

The greatest cause of Rainforest destruction today comes from human activities, which unlike natural damage, are unrelenting and thorough. Many of the effects from human-induced destruction of the rainforests are probably irreversible within our lifetime

XParrots has decided to combine art and NFT to help save the rainforests and in turn help its natural human and animal inhabitants. Our plan is to donate 5% of the profits from all NFTs sold by project XParrots. We will gradually transfer this donations to a transparent XRP address that will be published on the website.

All these funds from this address will go to one of the oldest and most trusted charities that accepts XRP as a crypto payment SAVE THE RAINFOREST .

All information will be transparently displayed on the website including audited confirmation that the charity has received the funds from the XParrots. The website will gradually show an online update of how much we have already prepared for the charity from NFT sales.
