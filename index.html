ABOUT XPARROTS

The XParrots will be a collection of 10,000 hand illustrated characters to be minted on the XRP ledger. Randomly assembled, every one of the XParrots will be exclusive, epic & unique! Each XParrot will be incredibly cool like their future owners.

VISION

Our vision for XParrots is not only to create an amazing and unique NFT integrated into the XRPL, but to make XParrots a launch pad in which we can release future NFT projects to build our community as we aim to create a metaverse.

We plan to collaborate with other NFT projects to become a core hub of the XRPL with an engaging and friendly community.

MISSION

Our mission is to build a community of like-minded people starting with the XParrots project. As a community member of XParrots you will have access to many unique and beneficial collections/projects that we plan to release in the future. Our plan is to deliver our roadmap and reward those members who stick with us as we grow within the XRPL ecosystem.
